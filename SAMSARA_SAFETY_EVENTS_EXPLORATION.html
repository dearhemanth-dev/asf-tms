⚙️ G-Force: The Real Metric (Not Arbitrary Labels)

Instead of guessing "high/moderate/low", we use physics:

G-Force Range Truck Experience Our Severity Manager Coaching
≥ 0.85G Emergency stop / ABS activation 🔴 HIGH "Emergency braking—improve planning/spacing"
0.65–0.85G Hard but controlled braking 🟡 MODERATE "Firm braking—smooth out transitions"
< 0.65G Normal/gentle braking 🟢 LOW "Good braking control"

Why G-force? It's objective physics. 0.87G means what it means everywhere—passenger comfort, brake wear, cargo shifting. No guessing. Manager immediately understands severity.
